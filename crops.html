Kharif (Monsoon)

Rice

Growing: Sow seeds in nurseries, transplant seedlings after 3-4 weeks. Requires flooded fields for optimal growth. Maintain water levels, control weeds, and use appropriate fertilizers. Harvest when grains are mature.

Maintenance: Regular irrigation, weed control, pest management, and timely fertilization.

Maize

Growing: Plant seeds in well-drained soil, provide adequate spacing. Fertilize with nitrogen and phosphorus, ensure sufficient irrigation, and control pests and diseases. Harvest when cobs are mature.

Maintenance: Irrigation, fertilization, pest and disease control, and weed management.

Cotton

Growing: Plant seeds in well-prepared soil. Provide adequate irrigation and nutrients. Manage pests like bollworms. Harvest mature cotton bolls.

Maintenance: Irrigation, fertilization, pest control, and weed management.

Rabi (Winter)

Wheat

Growing: Sow seeds in well-prepared soil, ensure adequate moisture. Fertilize with nitrogen and phosphorus, control weeds, and manage pests and diseases. Harvest when grains are mature and dry.

Maintenance: Irrigation, fertilization, weed and pest control.

Mustard

Growing: Plant seeds in well-drained soil, provide adequate spacing. Fertilize with nitrogen and phosphorus, control weeds, and manage pests and diseases. Harvest when pods are mature.

Maintenance: Irrigation, fertilization, weed and pest control.

Barley

Growing: Sow seeds in prepared soil. Irrigate as needed, fertilize, and control weeds. Harvest when grains are mature.

Maintenance: Irrigation, fertilization, and weed control.

Zaid (Summer)

Watermelon

Growing: Plant seeds in well-drained, sandy soil, provide ample sunlight. Fertilize with potassium and phosphorus, ensure regular irrigation, and control pests and diseases. Harvest when fruits are mature.

Maintenance: Irrigation, fertilization, and pest control.

Cucumber

Growing: Plant seeds in well-drained soil, provide support for vines. Fertilize with nitrogen and potassium, ensure regular irrigation, and control pests and diseases. Harvest when fruits are mature.

Maintenance: Irrigation, fertilization, and pest control.

Muskmelon

Growing: Sow seeds in warm, well-drained soil. Provide adequate sunlight and water. Harvest when fruits are fragrant and slip easily from the vine.

Maintenance: Regular irrigation, pest and disease management.
