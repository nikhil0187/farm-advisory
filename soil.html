Alluvial Soil

Rice

Needs: High water holding capacity, rich in potash, well-drained.

Care: Regular flooding, nutrient management, pest control.

Water: High.

Season: Kharif (Monsoon).

Expense: Moderate to high (irrigation, fertilizer).

Sugarcane

Needs: Deep, fertile soil, good drainage.

Care: Regular irrigation, nutrient management, pest control.

Water: High.

Season: Year-round (tropical/subtropical).

Expense: High (irrigation, processing).

Black Soil (Regur)

Cotton

Needs: High water retention, rich in lime, magnesium, and iron.

Care: Proper drainage, pest control, timely harvesting.

Water: Moderate.

Season: Kharif (Monsoon).

Expense: Moderate (pest control, irrigation).

Soybean

Needs: Well-drained, fertile soil.

Care: Weed control, disease management, proper spacing.

Water: Moderate.

Season: Kharif (Monsoon).

Expense: Moderate (seeds, fertilizer).

Red Soil

Groundnut

Needs: Well-drained, sandy loam to clay loam.

Care: Proper drainage, calcium application, pest control.

Water: Moderate.

Season: Kharif (Monsoon).

Expense: Moderate (seeds, calcium).

Millets (Ragi)

Needs: Well-drained, less fertile soil.

Care: Minimal irrigation, weed control.

Water: Low.

Season: Kharif (Monsoon).

Expense: Low (minimal inputs).

Laterite Soil

Cashew

Needs: Well-drained, acidic soil.

Care: Proper drainage, nutrient management, pest control.

Water: Moderate.

Season: Tropical.

Expense: Moderate to high (establishment, maintenance).

Tea

Needs: Acidic, well-drained soil, high rainfall.

Care: Shade management, pruning, regular plucking.

Water: High.

Season: Tropical/subtropical.

Expense: High (labor, processing).

Arid Soil

Millets (Bajra)

Needs: Sandy, low water holding capacity.

Care: Minimal irrigation, drought-resistant varieties.

Water: Very low.

Season: Kharif (Monsoon).

Expense: Low (minimal inputs).

Dates

Needs: Sandy, well-drained soil, high sunlight.

Care: Irrigation during establishment, pollination, pest control.

Water: Low to moderate.

Season: Arid/semi-arid.

Expense: Moderate (irrigation, labor).
